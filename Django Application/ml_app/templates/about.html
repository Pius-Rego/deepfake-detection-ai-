Why now

Narrative control is won or lost in the first minutes of a synthetic clip.

Operation Sindoor proved the gap.
Manual review cannot keep pace when fake statements, surrender videos, or diplomatic “apology” clips are released in waves.
Deepfake literacy is still low.
Detection must move upstream into institutions instead of expecting every citizen or journalist to self-verify perfectly.
Defence and public information teams need one surface.
DRISHTI combines model verdicts, identity matching, context scoring, and an analyst playbook in a single queue.
